Houping Xiao

 

Houping Xiao is a tenure track assistant professor at J. Mack Robinson College of Business, Georgia State University. He got his PhD in Computer Science and Engineering from the State University of New York at Buffalo under Dr. Jing Gao. He also got his bachelor of science degree in Statistics from Beijing Normal University.
